About Dr. Navya Raghuramu

Dr. Navya Raghuramu is a highly skilled and qualified Ayurvedic practitioner, with a passion for providing authentic Ayurvedic treatments to patients. Ayurveda, known as "The Science of Life," is an ancient yet highly relevant medical discipline that addresses various health challenges faced by individuals in today's world. Dr. Navya firmly believes that Ayurveda not only aims to cure diseases but also emphasizes the significance of food and lifestyle modifications necessary for maintaining overall well-being.

Dr. Navya holds a Bachelor's degree from Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and has significant experience as an Ayurvedic consultant, offering both online and offline consultations. Her contributions to research projects sanctioned by the Ayush Ministry and Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences highlight her commitment to the field of Ayurveda. Additionally, her scholarly publications in reputable national and international journals like IJRAP, IJRAR, and local magazines reflect her profound dedication and knowledge of Ayurvedic principles and practices.

Dr. Navya Raghuramu is has established Ambuja Ayurveda Chikitsa Kendra, with a vision to provide authentic Ayurvedic treatments and ensure that patients receive the best possible care in line with Ayurvedic principles. Her goal is to create a renowned institution that will serve the community by offering high-quality healthcare and medicines based on traditional classical guidelines.
